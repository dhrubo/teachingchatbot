🎨 Background color — 3 directions

Let me show different background approaches. Each shows the same layout with different background treatment:

A

Warm mesh gradient

Full-page rich gradient with soft color blobs. Coral top-left, amber center, violet bottom-right. Cards stay white/glass. Background feels alive.

Pros

  • Warm, energetic
  • Modern Duolingo feel
  • Cards pop on top
B

Deep violet night

Dark violet-indigo background (like late sunset). Cards are dark glass with subtle glow borders. Amber/coral accents pop. Futuristic study lounge vibe.

Pros

  • Striking, unique
  • Teen-appealing
  • Violet primary = strong brand

Cons

  • Dark default might polarize
  • Less "learning" warmth
C

Sunset bloom

Warm coral/amber gradient from top, fading to soft cream at bottom. The mascot sits in a large glowing halo. Think sunrise study session — bright, warm, inviting.

Pros

  • Bright and uplifting
  • Natural sunrise feel
  • Easy on the eyes
