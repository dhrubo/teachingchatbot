🧠 SARA Mascot — visual direction

The spec describes: modern hoodie/jacket, headset or AI halo, veena-inspired stylus, glowing aura, subtle bindu/spark, maths symbols floating. Which elements should the SVG prioritize?

A

Minimal geometric

Abstract humanoid shape with a glowing headpiece, stylus pen, floating π and ∑ symbols. Modern gradient fills. No facial features — like a constellation person. Techie, mysterious, cool.

. ★ /|\ ~glow~ / \ ∑ π √ stylus

Pros

  • Fast to build
  • No gender/age issues
  • Futuristic vibe

Cons

  • Less warm/personable
  • Might feel cold
B

Character illustration

A stylized character with a hoodie, glowing aura, headset, and a stylus. Warm smile, friendly eyes. A small bindu/dot on forehead as a subtle nod to Saraswati. Think Discord avatar meets study streamer.

(◕‿◕) /[hoodie]\ \ stylus / ∑ π √

Pros

  • Warm and relatable
  • Teen-appealing
  • Strong brand identity

Cons

  • Harder to get right in SVG
  • More complex code
C

Hybrid: Glowing orb + floating elements

A glowing coral/amber orb (like a friendly AI sun) surrounded by floatings math symbols (π, ∑, √, %) and a stylus/pen shape. A subtle crescent or bindu spark in the center. No human form — pure abstract intelligence.

π ∑ (●) √ % ╱ stylus

Pros

  • Unique, memorable
  • No human representation issues
  • Beautiful glowing effects

Cons

  • Less "Saraswati" connection
  • Less personable

I think A (minimal geometric) would look great, but B (character illustration) has more personality for teens. What's your preference?
