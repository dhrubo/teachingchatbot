🎨 Color palette direction

Current theme is sunset coral/amber. The spec asks to add purple/violet. Here are 2 palette directions:

A

Extended sunset

Keep the current coral/amber as primary. Add violet as a secondary accent for badges, highlights, and the mascot's glow. Cream/off-white for backgrounds.

orange amber violet purple cream dark

Pros

  • Minimal CSS changes
  • Instantly familiar
  • Violet adds freshness
B

Deep space sunset

Shift primary to deep violet/indigo, with coral/amber as accent highlights. Dark mode becomes true dark (navy-black). Glowing neon accents. More dramatic and futuristic.

violet indigo orange amber dark light

Pros

  • More distinctive
  • Techie/futuristic vibe
  • Stronger brand identity

Cons

  • CSS overhaul needed
  • Loses warm tutor feel

I'd recommend A — keeps what works, adds violet where it matters (mascot glow, badges, highlights). Less risk, less work.
