1. Core App Layout Structure

How should SARA guide the student through lessons and challenges?

A

Floating Central Product Layout (Recommended)

A centered, high-focus learning area with modern translucent panels and a contextual progress/action footer. Hides chat-like history on active missions. Feels like Brilliant/Duolingo.

Nav: Topic Picker & sound/account dropdowns
Center: Interactive large concept slides or fullscreen challenges
Footer: Clear "Continue", "Challenge Mode" buttons
B

Sidebar Navigation & Grid Layout

A multi-column workspace with a pinned sidebar on the left and a grid-based progress tracker in the center. Active lessons split screen between materials and chat-assistant feedback.

Sidebar: Full menu of Year 8/9 math topics always visible
Center: Split learning panel (diagrams left, conversation right)
Footer: Standard next action bar
